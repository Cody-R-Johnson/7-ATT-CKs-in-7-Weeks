# Rules of Engagement

## Authorized Scope

- `http://127.0.0.1:8000`
- Files inside this repository.
- Logs generated by the local ACME app.

## Out of Scope

- Public internet targets.
- Real ACME-related companies or domains.
- Systems owned by employers, schools, cloud providers, or other third parties unless explicitly authorized in writing.
- Denial-of-service testing outside a disposable local lab.

## Evidence Handling

Save evidence under the matching weekly `evidence/` directory. Prefer sanitized notes and screenshots. Do not store real credentials, secrets, or customer data in this repository.

## Stop Conditions

Stop testing if you accidentally point tools at a non-local host, generate unexpected destructive behavior, or discover real sensitive data.
